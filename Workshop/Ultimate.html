My Ulitmate Workshop

I may have started from humble beginnings but a girls got to dream. One day I hope to have all the tools listed below because I think that they would make up one ultimate workshop. If you want to help me reach my dreams then make sure you watch my videos to help me grow my channel.

Current tools

Mitre saw

My first major power tool purchase will always have a special place in my heart and my workshop.

Drill and Driver

An essential item that I couldn't imagine a workshop without.

Router and Router Lift

This router is amazing. Enough said.

Kreg jig

Quick and easy joinery method is a great addition to my ultimate shop.

Tools currently on my wish list

Thicknesser

My next goal for my shop is to be able to make wood in any thickness for different projects.

Band Saw

The ability to rip stock and create curves will be a great addition to my workshop

Jointer

Make edge jointing and squaring up wood easy with this tool.

Track saw

An alternative to getting a table saw is using a track saw to rip down sheet goods.

Possible tools for the future

Nail and staple gun

I want to add this to my shop for quick and easy tacking before screwing pieces together.

Sander (belt/spindle/disk)

A sander where i can take the piece to the sander is what I'm looking for here.

Sawstop table saw

If i ever buy a table saw it will always be a sawstop due to the breaking system.

Plunge router

Replacing my handheld router with something that can do plunge routing as well would be great.

Tools that I wont be purchasing

Some tools I don't plan on buying. These in include things like a drill press or a lathe. A drill press for instance is very space consuming for the functionality it provides. A lathe is too expensive and requires a special set of accessories to get the best use out of it. I also don't plan on investing in hand tools like hand planes. I don't have the strength or the passion for hand tools to learn the skill. I greatly respect those who do but I have made the decsion not to invest in handtools for my shop.
